Herne Bay & Villages Prayer Guide

Beltinge, Broomfield, Greenhill, Hampton, Herne, Herne Bay, Hoath, Marshside and Reculver

Commit to the Lord whatever you do, and he will establish your plans.
Proverbs 16:3

Wednesday
1 February		 We pray for Vanessa who begins today as a part-time Youth & Twenties worker at Beacon Church. May she be blessed and excited by this new role as she reaches out to young people in and around the area.
Thursday
2 February		 Many of our young people travel to Canterbury for further education and we pray today for the Simon Langton Boys School. The boiler has broken down and using electric heating on this scale causes power failures meaning home learning is the only option.
Friday
3 February		 Our prayers today are for the management, staff and residents at Strode Park. Following a recent spot check the main care home and Coach House have been placed 'in special measures' and ordered to improve. This is an organisation that has always been loved.
Saturday
4 February		 Messy Church is being held today at HB Baptist church and so we pray for the children and parents who will be coming to enjoy activities and sharing food. As they have fun and enjoy the time together, we pray that they will learn about Jesus.
Sunday
5 February		 "..and on this rock I will build my church and the gates of Hades will not overcome it."
Matthew 16:18
Monday
6 February		 Today marks the start of Childrenâ€™s Mental Health Week. Many young people are still struggling mentally with isolation issues caused by lockdown and we pray as teaching unions plan strike action that God will move with power to protect young lives.
Tuesday
7 February		 We think of the friendship group CAMEO (come and meet each other) as they gather together this afternoon at HB Baptist Church and we pray that new friendships will be made and that those who live alone will find comfort in the company of others.
Wednesday
8 February		 As the team meet this evening to pray and finalise plans for Christians Together monthly Pray for the Bay prayer meetings in central Herne Bay, we pray that they will be led and guided by the Holy Spirit and blessed by the love of Jesus.
Thursday
9 February		 We ask God to send his gift of peace to all those we know, and to people across the world who are suffering. Pray that they will be given strength to cope with the many challenges they face and courage to overcome them.
Friday
10 February		 Prayers today are for Reflections Coffee Morning at HB Baptist church in the High Street. May all those visiting the project this morning will be blessed and feel the presence and the peace of God in the building.
Saturday
11 February		 We continue to pray for what has become the "God space" in Mortimer Street every Saturday. The Christian market stall, the "Saved by the Book" tables where Christian books are given free and United Church's open doors and coffee. We praise God!
Sunday
12 February		 Guard yourselves and all the flock of which the Holy Spirit has made you overseers. Be shepherds of the church of God which he bought with his own blood.
Acts 20:28
Monday
13 February		 It's half-term break for schools in this area and we pray that it will be a fun, restful space for students. Many will be studying for GCSE exams so we pray that they will still be able to make time to relax. Pray for parents who work and plan around the week.
Tuesday
14 February		 Today is Valentine's Day and we pray for all those who do not feel loved or those for whom their relationship is difficult to sustain. Help to recognise their loneliness and may we be led to offer friendship and comfort, reflecting the love of Jesus.
Wednesday
15 February		 Pray for the many care homes in and around Herne Bay. Recruitment of staff has been a real problem for many homes and we ask that more people of all ages will feel called to this amazing work.
Thursday
16 February		 Pray for those who need to travel to work each day and are trying to cope with rail strikes and cancellations. Remember too all who need to to attend special hospital appointments outside the local area and face constant changes in bus timetables.
Friday
17 February		 We pray for our neighbours and those living in our road. If we have time today, we could try to do a prayer walk round our local area and pray for a blessing on each house that we pass.
Saturday
18 February		 We pray for doctors, nurses, pharmacists and all those working in every department of our National Health Service. As we begin to come out of the worst of winter flu and Covid, may stress levels improve and may recruitment of staff increase.
Sunday
19 February		 Since you are eager to have spiritual gifts, try to excel in gifts that build up the church.
1 Corinthians 14:12
Monday
20 February		 Prayers today are for the Coffee at the Mill project in Herne. This is a warm, happy space where friendships are being made, may all who spend time there feel loved and cared for. We pray for people who are isolated and lonely in our communities.
Tuesday
21 February		 As we celebrate Shrove Tuesday and make pancakes, we remember in our prayers that many will have very little food today. We pray for greater awareness of poverty in our midst and that help will be given to families trying to manage on very limited incomes.
Wednesday
22 February		 Ash Wednesday, the beginning of Lent, and we pray that for Christians it will be the start of 40 days of drawing closer and finding time to listen to God. Instead of just giving up biscuits this year, we pray for daily opportunities to show more love and support.
Thursday
23 February		 Pray for today's very popular Warm Space and Soup Lunch at the Salvation Army. As numbers increase each week, we pray that our Lord will provide enough helpers to cope and will give strength to all those involved in this lovely project.
Friday
24 February		 Please pray for reporters who work on the Herne Bay Gazette and presenters on Radio Kent. Give thanks for communications in difficult times and help us all to share positive news with our churches and our friends and neighbours.
Saturday
25 February		 Pray for house group and prayer group leaders in our churches. As we meet together in small groups, may each one of us feel the presence of Jesus in our midst. As we seek to learn more of his plan for our lives, may we support each other in prayer.
Sunday
26 February		 Then the Spirit lifted me up and brought me into the inner court and the glory of God filled the temple.
Ezekiel 43:5
Monday
27 February		 Apparently today is National Polar Bear Day! - however it does highlight the plight of many animals on our planet who are in danger of extinction. We pray that there will be greater awareness and action against the effects of climate change.
Tuesday
28 February		 As we begin to notice the days getting longer and Spring flowers appearing, we thank our God for the evidence of new birth around us. Help us to concentrate on the many blessings in our lives, even in the darkest times.
