Herne Bay & Villages Prayer Guide

Beltinge, Broomfield, Greenhill, Hampton, Herne, Herne Bay, Hoath, Marshside and Reculver

“The world is not what it was meant to be. What would it look like if we partner with God to change that?” Tearfund

Thursday
1 August		 During this holiday month we pray for each of the churches in our town and villages. (In alphabetical order!) Pray for leaders, for church families and for future growth in both spirituality and numbers. Pray too for Church Unity as we all proclaim the gospel.
Friday
2 August		 We pray for the 5 young people from Beacon Church who are at the Newday Festival, Norfolk Showground, near Norwich. The festival now gathers 7000 young people. May they have life-changing encounters and deepen their relationships in the youth group.
Saturday
3 August		 The prayer today is for the work and people of St Andrew’s, Hampton as they seek to serve the people of that area. We ask God to bless those who work there and ask for the Holy Spirit to be with them in their ongoing connections with Hampton School.
Sunday
4 August		 Yours, O Lord, is the greatness and the power and the glory and the majesty and the splendour, for everything in heaven and on earth is yours. Yours, O Lord, is the kingdom. You are exalted as head over all.
1 Chronicles 29:11
Monday
5 August		 We remember Sue, Simon and Steve and all those who worship at St Bartholomew’s Church. Pray that God will provide richly to cover the much-needed repairs to the church building. Pray too for Ken Skinner as he leads their amazing worship band.
Tuesday
6 August		 Pray for the Bay Ignite - 3.35pm ‘til 5.15 at St Andrews, Hampton. Pray that good relationships and friendships will develop as the Gospel is shared. Let us ask God to oversee our practical and prayerful help to those most in need in our communities.
Wednesday
7 August		 Pray for Food and Fellowship at St Andrews today – an opportunity for the community to share a meal together. May it be a time of fun and friendship for all. Pray too for preparations for the Activity Days at St Peter’s Greenhill for 4 Fridays this month. May God bless the events and bring lots of families along each week.
Thursday
8 August		 Pray for the Beacon Church and all who worship regularly there. Prayers particularly for Steve Dunn who is on a 3 month-sabbatical. May he be blessed with times of refreshing during his break.
Friday
9 August		 Give thanks for the 8 new Street Pastors being trained for Herne Bay – God is indeed good all the time! We now need more people to volunteer as Prayer Pastors to support this amazing work each Friday evening.
Saturday
10 August		 We pray for all those at Beltinge Baptist Church as they work to bring the gospel to the people of Beltinge. May God inspire and lead them in the coming months. Pray for all those who have concerns about the plans for major housebuilding projects in the area.
Sunday
11 August		 If any of you is lacking in wisdom, ask God, who gives to all generously and ungrudgingly, and it will be given to you. But ask in faith never doubting, for the one who doubts is like a wave of the sea tossed by the wind.
James 1: 5-6
Monday
12 August		 Pray for Janet at Broomfield URC Church. Thank God for the beautiful building that is now being used as a centre both for worship and fellowship for the people in and around Broomfield. Pray for the groups who meet there during the week.
Tuesday
13 August		 We pray for Canopy Church - the members, many of whom live in the Bay, meet regularly for worship in Greenhill and also work together to offer coffee and support for those experiencing difficult times and situations in Canterbury.
Wednesday
14 August		 We remember all those who are part of the leadership team at Christ Church. Pray that they will be able to take time to rest this month and that the Autumn will be an exciting time for them with all their mission initiatives bearing much fruit in our town .
Thursday
15 August		 Pray for the Ignite social this evening and on the 29th. Give thanks for the 34 unchurched adults who came to the last social and pray that numbers will be good again and lives will be changed. Bless all those who work to make these events so welcoming.
Friday
16 August		 We pray that HB Baptist Church in the busy centre of Herne Bay will attract many visitors during the various exhibitions being held there in the next 3 weeks. Pray that those visiting the church will be blessed by the Spirit of God as they enter.
Saturday
17 August		 Our prayers today are for Holy Cross at Hoath we remember and pray for all those who work tirelessly with Messy Church, Prayer Breakfasts and many events throughout the year as they serve their rural community.
Sunday
18 August		 Two are better than one because they have a good reward for their toil. For if they fall, one will lift up the other; but woe to one who is alone and falls and does not have another to help.
Ecclesiastes 4:9-10
Monday
19 August		 We remember today the Methodist Chapel at Marshside. This small building stands as a testament to God’s presence in that hamlet and we ask God to bless all those who visit and spend time in worship there.
Tuesday
20 August		 Pray for Carol, Cat and Stacey at St Martin’s Church in Herne. Give them strength and wisdom as they plan ways to grow in mission and outreach in the months ahead. Pray too that the final phase of work on this amazing church is completed safely this Autumn.
Wednesday
21 August		 Prayers today are for the visit to Whitebirch Care Home by Christ Church members. As they offer their ‘Songs of Praise’ may this be a good time to share memories of the well-remembered hymns and an opportunity for people to share stories of their faith..
Thursday
22 August		 Our prayers are for a happy event at Canterbury Garden Centre organised by Christ Church and St Andrew’s – a ladies cream tea and a short talk with a Christian theme. May many ladies bring friends to share in the fun and also to hear the Message.
Friday
23 August		 Prayers today are for the people of St Mary’s, Reculver – for their work with the neighbouring school and all they do in the community.
Saturday
24 August		 Our thoughts and prayers are with Father Mark at Our Lady of the Sacred Heart in Herne Bay. Thank God for his steadfast presence in the Bay and for hosting the Good Friday Walk of Witness each year – may he be blessed and energised in all he does.
Sunday
25 August		 Working in a team can try the patience at times but the wise person will avoid losing their temper to avoid unpleasant ramifications.
Proverbs 25:28
Monday
26 August		 Ask God to make his presence known in a special way in Greenhill this month. We remember the weekly groups who meet in St Peter’s Church and those who work with the monthly Messy Church and holiday activities.
Tuesday
27 August		 Pray for good health and protection for Josh and Cat at Bay Vineyard. May their work bring forth much fruit as they seek to bring new people to the Lord.
Wednesday
28 August		 Pray for Riverside Vineyard Church and their committed prayers each month for Herne Bay. Thank God for their generosity and vision as they encourage people from all denominations to be involved with the monthly Prayer Space at the Sailing Club.
Thursday
29 August		 Pray for Vicki and her family at the Salvation Army. Thank God for all her hard work and ask that the corps might grow in numbers and faith. Blessings on all who worship there as they welcome everyone to the regular events during the week.
Friday
30 August		 We remember the prayerful people of Sunnyhill Evangelical Church. May God be with them and guide them in all they do. Pray this evening for safety and health for our loyal, committed group of Street Pastors.
Saturday
31 August		 For the United Church in Mortimer Street, we ask God to give wisdom as they cope with decisions about the future of the building.
