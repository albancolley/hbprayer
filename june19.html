Herne Bay & Villages Prayer Guide

Beltinge, Broomfield, Greenhill, Hampton, Herne, Herne Bay, Hoath, Marshside and Reculver

"Prayer is where the action is" John Wesley

Saturday
1 June		 Today we pray for the gazebo outside the United Church in Mortimer Street inviting people to write a prayer and peg it to the railings. 9am-1pm. We ask God to bless the celebration of the 140th anniversary of HB Baptist Church held at 7pm in Christ Church.
Sunday
2 June		 Prayers and Praise for our friends at Beacon Church who are today moving to bigger premises at ‘Vibe’ on the Herne Bay seafront for their Sunday worship. It’s not about buildings – this is about people!
Monday
3 June		 Give thanks and pray for the 9am Novena Prayer time at St Mary’s, Reculver.
Pray for safety and energy for Darran & Denise from Christ Church as they lead special events throughout this week in the global wave of prayer ‘Thy Kingdom Come’.
Tuesday
4 June		 For those who are unable to join in the events this week, please pray at home or work.
Pray for our town and for more people to come to know Jesus.
Remember in our prayers the 2pm Novena Prayer time at Holy Cross, Hoath.
Wednesday
5 June		 May God pour his blessings on the Food and Fellowship event at Christ Church and speaker Archdeacon Jo Kelly-Moore. A time for everyone to come together. Pray that we will be brave and take a friend to hear the gospel message.
Thursday
6 June		 Pray for safety for tomorrow’s Prayer Walk leaving at 5.30pm outside Christ Church, William Street. May the Holy Spirit move across our town as we walk the streets.
Please pray too for Novena Prayer space at 9am St Bartholomew’s Church.
Friday
7 June		 24 hours of prayer begins today, 5.30pm at St Andrews, Hampton. Pray that this will be supported by people from all our churches. Expect miracles. All are invited to an open meeting with Prayer Pastors at the Baptist Friendship Centre 7.30-8.30p.m – do come.
Saturday
8 June		 We pray for our children and remember Herne Summer Fair with Herne Schools and Herne Church working together to make this a wonderful day for people of all ages. We thank God for those volunteers who give their time so freely.
Sunday
9 June		 They saw what seemed to be tongues of fire that came to rest on each of them. All were filled with the Holy Spirit and began to speak in other tongues. Acts 2:3
Pray for the Holy Spirit afternoon event and praise at St Bartholomew’s. 2pm-7pm.
Monday
10 June		 Pray for the PCC members of St Martin’s Church, Herne as they meet this evening to pray, listen to God and make plans to fulfil the vision ‘To share God’s love and grow God’s people’. May our Lord bless their work across the whole parish.
Tuesday
11 June		 We ask God for a fun and learning time at Messy Church, Reculver School 3.20-5pm. Blessings on parents and children. Hold the volunteers in your prayers as they seek to do God’s will.
Wednesday
12 June		 Salvation Army’s new project ‘Coffee on Wednesday’ in various coffee shops around Herne Bay (venues to be confirmed) Pray that women both Christian and non-Christian will respond to the invitation to meet together.
Thursday
13 June		 We pray today for all those involved in youth work, both in our churches and in the community. Remember in our prayers those volunteers who lead the Scouts, Guides and Beavers and those teaching life skills and ways of coping with life today.
Friday
14 June		 Our Prayer Pastors will be out this evening walking the seafront and parks of Herne Bay. Thank God for new volunteers and pray for safety and opportunities for these amazing people to make meaningful contacts tonight with people in need.
Saturday
15 June		 Remember all those at the 8am Prayer Breakfast at St Mary’s Reculver as they meet in fellowship. May it be a time of prayerful fun as they meet with Jesus. Our prayers too for Messy Church at St Peter’s, Greenhill today 4-6pm. May the children be blessed.
Sunday
16 June		 The body is a unit, though it is made up of many parts; and though all its parts are many, they form one body. So it is with Christ. For we were all baptised by one Spirit into one body – whether Jews or Greeks, slave or free. 1 Corinthians 12:12
Monday
17 June		 Prayers for KEC Care Home as representatives from the Salvation Army take the Word in to residents. Remember all those who live and work in our rest homes that they may be beacons of hope and love for carers and those being cared for.
Tuesday
18 June		 Wherever you are this evening, join in prayer with those at the Salvation Army Prayer Space 7-8.30pm. Pray for Vicki Tate and her family and for the faithful people at the Herne Bay Corps.
Wednesday
19 June		 Pray for those suffering from anxiety and depression and for those who support them through this illness. We ask God to be with counsellors and mental health workers as they seek to offer patient understanding and practical help to their clients.
Thursday
20 June		 Tomorrow is the summer equinox and we thank God for the summertime and pray for those working hard to promote awareness of climate change. Pray that in our lives we will play our part in respecting and caring for God’s creation.
Friday
21 June		 Pray for Beacon Church as they join with the global family for a prayer event this evening entitled ‘ENOUGH’ They will be praying about things we’ve seen and ‘had enough of’ to our Father who is ‘more than enough’.
Saturday
22 June		 As St Bartholomew’s hold a ‘Gin, Juice and Jazz’ event, we pray that this will not only be a fundraiser but also a mission opportunity showing that Jesus came to bring us ‘life in all its fullness’.
Sunday
23 June		 We pray for good weather and people with open hearts to enjoy the music and hear the Word this afternoon at our United Songs of Praise – 3pm in the Bandstand Pray for those involved in 6pm Evening Praise for everyone at St Bartholomew’s.
Monday
24 June		 Pray for those visiting Heydon Meyer Care Home today, may their time there be a real blessing to everyone in that place.
Riverside Vineyard lead a prayer evening at Herne Bay Sailing Club 7.30-8.30pm.
Tuesday
25 June		 Remember today all those who are struggling to pay their bills and feed their families. Pray that Our Lord will be with all those at the Food Bank this morning at the Salvation Army in Herne Bay. Thank God for food collections in supermarkets and churches.
Wednesday
26 June		 Pray for leaders of our churches across Herne Bay and local villages. May they be filled with the Holy Spirit as were the early disciples. Pray for health for them and their families and may they be protected under the shadow of his wing.
Thursday
27 June		 Remember David and June as they lead worship and word at Tarry’s Care Home today. We thank God for this ministry and ask God to bless those who are considering entering care as the next chapter of their lives.
Friday
28 June		 Our prayers today are for Hospice nurses and volunteers. Ask God to be with those who are facing terminal illness and families who are trying to cope at this difficult time. Pray that pain will be eased and peace will be with those who mourn.
Saturday
29 June		 Pray for holidaymakers and visitors to our town and villages. May they be made welcome and enjoy a time of rest and space from busy lives. For those who seek a place of worship while away, may they be blessed with warmth and love everywhere.
Sunday
30 June		 I urge then, first of all, that petitions, prayers, intercession and thanksgiving be made for all people – for kings and all those in authority, that we may live peaceful and quiet lives in all godliness and holiness. This is good and pleases God our Saviour who wants all people to be saved. 1 Timothy 2: 1-4
